English

SkinwellAI ("we", "us") provides a cosmetic skin- and facial-appearance analysis

app. This policy explains what we collect, why, who processes it, and the rights

you have. SkinwellAI is a wellness product, not a medical device — see our

Terms.

1. What we collect and why

  • Face photo (selfie you capture) — to compute visual appearance estimates.

Processed only by our own backend computer vision (MediaPipe/OpenCV).

Stored privately (access only via short-lived signed URLs), **retained for at

most 30 days, and deleted when you delete your account**.

  • Derived skin metrics & aesthetic scores — numeric estimates computed from

your photo (e.g. hydration appearance, redness estimate). Stored with your

account; anonymized on deletion.

  • AI narrative text — an optional written summary of your scores.
  • Health data (Apple Health / Health Connect) — sleep, heart-rate

variability (HRV), resting heart rate, and mood, *only if you connect it*.

This data is read on your device only, used to show patterns in your Skin

Journal, and is **never sent to our servers, never shared, and never used for

advertising**. You can disconnect at any time.

  • Account & identity — email and, if you use Sign in with Apple/Google, the

provider's user identifier. Phone number if you use phone sign-in.

  • Subscription status & purchase events — to unlock paid features.
  • Device push token — to send reminders you enable.
  • Usage analytics — only if you allow it; used to improve the product.
  • Crash/diagnostics — pseudonymous stability data.

2. The face photo is NOT sent to OpenAI

Your face image never leaves our own systems for AI narrative generation.

When you explicitly opt in to the AI narrative, we send **only the derived

numeric scores and a skin-profile label, as text — no image** — to our LLM

provider (OpenAI). If you do not opt in, a narrative is generated on our own

servers with no third-party AI. You can withdraw this consent at any time in

Profile → Privacy & AI. See the AI Data Processing page for detail.

3. Who processes your data (sub-processors)

  • OpenAI — derived numeric scores (text only, no image), AI narrative,

consent-gated; not used for advertising; not used to train models by default.

  • RevenueCat / Apple — subscription and billing management.
  • Sentry — crash and diagnostics.
  • Expo Push / Apple Push (APNs) — notification delivery.
  • Cloud hosting + object storage — running the service and storing images

privately. All transfers use HTTPS/TLS.

4. What we do NOT do

We do not track you across other apps or websites, do not use

advertising SDKs, and do not sell your personal data.

5. Retention & deletion

Raw scan images: ≤30 days (and removed on deletion). Account data: kept until you

delete your account, then erased or anonymized. Deletion is available in-app

(Profile → Delete Account) and described on the Account Deletion page.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live (incl. GDPR/KVKK), you may access, correct, export,

or delete your data, and withdraw consent. Use in-app Export my data and

Delete Account, or contact Demircan Yılmaz — support@skinwellai.com.

7. Children

SkinwellAI is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your

region). We do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Security

Encryption in transit (TLS); images stored privately with signed-URL access only

and provider-managed encryption at rest.

9. International transfers

Processors may operate outside your country; transfers use appropriate

safeguards.

10. Changes

We will update the effective date and, for material changes, prompt you in-app

(including re-consent for AI processing).

kimliği; telefonla giriş kullanırsanız telefon numarası.

  • Abonelik durumu ve satın alma olayları — ücretli özellikleri açmak için.
  • Cihaz bildirim jetonu — açtığınız hatırlatıcılar için.
  • Kullanım analitiği — yalnızca izin verirseniz; ürünü iyileştirmek için.
  • Çökme/teşhis — takma adlı kararlılık verisi.

2. Yüz fotoğrafı OpenAI'a GÖNDERİLMEZ

Yüz görüntünüz, AI anlatı üretimi için **sistemlerimizden hiçbir zaman

çıkmaz. AI anlatısını açıkça onayladığınızda**, AI sağlayıcımıza (OpenAI)

sayfasına bakın.
